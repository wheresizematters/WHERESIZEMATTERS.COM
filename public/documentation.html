Getting Started

Create your account and get verified in minutes.

Creating an Account

Sign up at wheresizematters.com using one of three methods:

Email — Enter your email and set a password. You'll receive a verification link to confirm your address.

X / Twitter — Tap "Sign in with X" to authenticate via OAuth. Your X handle is linked to your SIZE profile automatically.

Crypto Wallet — Connect a wallet (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, or any WalletConnect-compatible wallet) and sign a message to verify ownership.

Setting Up Your Profile

Once signed in, complete your profile:

Username — Choose a unique handle. This is how others find you on the leaderboard and in DMs.

Size — Self-report your size or skip this step until you're ready to verify.

Bio — A short description that appears on your profile card. Premium users can add clickable links.

Getting Verified

Verification uses AI-powered image analysis. Upload a photo with a reference object (ruler, credit card, or dollar bill). Claude Vision AI performs a two-pass measurement with girth cross-check.

Possible outcomes:

Auto-verified — AI confirms your measurement. You earn 0.001% $SIZE, a verified checkmark badge, and leaderboard eligibility.

Pending — Edge case. Queued for manual review.

Rejected — Photo didn't meet requirements. You can resubmit.

Verification photos are deleted after review. They are never stored permanently or shared.
02

Feed & Posting

Share, discuss, and discover content.

Creating Posts

Tap the compose button to create a new post. Three post types are available:

Discussion — Text-based posts with optional tags. The default format for sharing thoughts, questions, and updates.

Poll — Create a poll with up to four options. Results are visible in real time.

Media — Attach images to your post. Available to Premium and verified users. Media is hosted on S3 with signed URLs.

Voting & Commenting

Every post supports upvotes and downvotes. Upvoting quality content earns both you and the author $SIZE tokens. Comments are threaded and support the same voting system.

Tags & Content Discovery

Tag your posts with relevant topics to help others find your content. The feed supports filtering by tag, trending topics, and a "Nearby" view powered by geolocation. Free users see a paywall after every 5 posts.

04

Direct Messaging

Private 1:1 conversations with ephemeral media.

Starting Conversations

Tap any user's profile to open a DM thread. Messages are delivered in real time. You can message any user on the platform — no mutual follow required.

Media Sharing

Send photos and media in DMs. All media is served via ephemeral signed URLs that expire after 5 minutes — Snapchat-style. Once the URL expires, the media is no longer accessible.

Read Receipts

DMs include read receipts so you always know when your message has been seen. Receipts appear as a subtle checkmark beneath the message.

Daily Fee Distribution

Fees from all $SIZE trades on Uniswap V4 are collected and distributed once daily. 75% goes to stakers (proportional to effective stake), 25% goes to the protocol. Your effective stake is calculated as:

effective_stake = tokens_staked × tier_boost

APY is variable and driven entirely by trading volume. No emissions, no team unlocks — staker yield is a pure function of market activity.

FAQ

Common questions, straight answers.

Is this real?

Yes. SIZE. is live at wheresizematters.com. The app is a production PWA on Base with real users, real tokens, and real staking.

Is my data safe?

Verification photos are deleted immediately after AI review. They are never stored permanently, shared with third parties, or used for any purpose beyond measurement verification.

What chain is $SIZE on?

Base — an Ethereum Layer 2 built by Coinbase. Low fees, fast transactions, full EVM compatibility.

How do I earn $SIZE?

Use the app. Every action earns proportional rewards — logging in, posting, receiving upvotes, sending messages, referring users, and verifying your size. The more active you are, the more you earn.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. All your data, posts, and profile information will be permanently removed.
