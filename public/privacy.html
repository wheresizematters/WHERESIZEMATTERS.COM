Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

SIZE. ("we", "our", "us") operates the website wheresizematters.com. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our service.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Email address, username, and profile data you provide when creating an account.
  • OAuth data: When you sign in with Google or X (Twitter), we receive your public profile information (name, profile picture, email) from those services.
  • Verification photos: Photos submitted for size verification are processed by AI and deleted after review. They are never shared publicly.
  • Wallet addresses: If you connect a crypto wallet, we store the public address. We never access your private keys.
  • Usage data: Posts, messages, votes, and other interactions within the platform.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the service
  • To verify your identity and size through our AI verification system
  • To display your profile, posts, and rankings on the platform
  • To process $SIZE token rewards and staking
  • To send notifications about your account activity
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and sybil attacks

Data Storage

Your data is stored on AWS (Amazon Web Services) infrastructure in the United States. We use DynamoDB for structured data and S3 for media storage. All data is encrypted at rest.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service providers (AWS, Stripe) necessary to operate the platform
  • Law enforcement when required by law

Your public profile information (username, size tier, verification status) is visible to other users by design.

Verification Photos

Photos submitted for size verification are processed by Claude AI (Anthropic) for measurement analysis. Photos that pass auto-verification are immediately deleted. Photos requiring manual review are stored temporarily and deleted after admin review. Verification photos are never displayed publicly.

Blockchain Data

If you interact with $SIZE tokens, DickCoins, or staking contracts, your wallet address and transaction data are recorded on the Base blockchain (Ethereum L2) and are publicly visible by nature of blockchain technology.

Your Rights

  • You can delete your account at any time from the Settings page
  • You can request a copy of your data by contacting us
  • You can disconnect your wallet at any time

Cookies

We use localStorage to store your authentication token. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

Age Requirement

You must be at least 18 years old to use SIZE.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

Contact
