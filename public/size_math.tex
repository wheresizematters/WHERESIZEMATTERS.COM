\section*{Staker and Epoch Pool}

\[
\begin{aligned}
R_{\text{stakers}} &= F_{\text{protocol}} \times 0.75 \\[10pt]
R_{\text{epoch}}   &= F_{\text{protocol}} \times 0.25
\end{aligned}
\]

% ─────────────────────────────────────────
\section*{Staker Pool and Epoch Pool from Volume}

Given daily trading volume $V$ and \$SIZE price $P_S$:

\[
\begin{aligned}
\text{staker pool} &= \frac{V \times 0.01 \times 0.099 \times 0.75}{P_S} \\[10pt]
\text{epoch pool}  &= \frac{V \times 0.01 \times 0.099 \times 0.25}{P_S}
\end{aligned}
\]
